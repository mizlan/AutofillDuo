AutofillDuo Activation

Instructions for Use

  1. At a Duo prompt, switch the default method to passcode
  2. In Duo device management, begin the process to add a new device
  3. When Duo asks, say you've already installed Duo Mobile on your mobile device
  4. When Duo asks for a phone number, say you have a tablet
  5. When the QR code appears, ask for an email to be sent instead
  6. Paste the link you got in your email below
